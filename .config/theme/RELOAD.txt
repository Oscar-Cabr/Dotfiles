The following are the neccesary commands, instructions to change theme colors:

Waybar
	  pkill waybar
	  waybar &
Kitty
    kitty @ set-colors -a
Hyprland
    (auto)
    (maybe hyprctl reload)
Eww
    eww reload
 or
    pkill eww
    eww daemon
    eww open left-bar
Starship
    (auto)
Wofi
    (auto, restart launcher)
fuzzel
    (auto, restart launcher)
Swaync
    pkill -USR1 swaync

The following are modules from where its necessary to change font:

Waybar, Eww (poweroff), fuzzel, wofi
